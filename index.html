About Me

I am a front-end web developer and aspiring web designer from San Diego, CA. Inspired by the intersection of art, culture and technology in everyday life. Looking forward to collaborating with other developers to craft web applications that are dynamic, useful, user-friendly, and aesthetically pleasing. Let’s connect!

Art

I've worked as a professional designer & production manager at a studio specializing in weddings & events. I love exploring concepts of color, form, texture, and proportion in my designs. Out of this love for aesthetics, a deep appreciation for well-designed products developed, and my passion for functionality and user experience evolved.

Culture

I've always been fascinated in understanding how people perceive the world, and how that perception influences their actions & decisions. Pursuing my bachelor's degree in Sociology has sharpened my ability to think critically about culture and the role it plays in our lives. I believe that good design reflects culture, even while continuing to shape it.

Technology

As a Bay Area/Silicon Valley native and daughter of two engineers, I'm no stranger to the culture & influence of technology. Having always been an avid consumer of tech, it came as some surprise that I didn't really pursue till 2016. I caught the coding bug so late in 2016. while attending LEARN Academy - a four-month, full-stack web development bootcamp in San Diego, CA. You can learn more about my skill stack below.
